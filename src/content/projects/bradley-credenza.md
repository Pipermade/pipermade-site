The Bradley Credenza is an original Pipermade design — a low cabinet that sets warm walnut fronts against a black stained maple case, lifted on a slender black base.

Every element is worked by hand. The walnut door and drawer fronts are laid up so the grain runs continuously across the piece, and each drawer is lined in pale maple, with a fitted valet tray that lifts out. A drop-front compartment lowers onto its own shelf, and the brass pulls are shaped individually — no two are quite identical.

We built it from the boards you'll see it standing in front of: rough walnut and maple, milled and joined in our Madison shop. It's the kind of piece the whole practice is built around — designed once, made by the same hands that finish and deliver it.
