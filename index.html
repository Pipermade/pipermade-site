Who We Are

About Pipermade

Pipermade is a small custom carpentry shop in Madison, Wisconsin, run by Luke Piper. We build high-end built-ins, cabinetry, and millwork the traditional way: measured on site, fabricated carefully in the shop, and installed by the same hands that built it.

We keep our crew small on purpose. It means the person you talk to about your project is the person cutting the joinery — and it means nothing leaves the shop that we wouldn't put in our own homes.
